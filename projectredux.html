Batman

The Dark Knight

Vigilante
Bruce Wayne

On a dark night, the Wayne family have just finished watching The Mask of Zorro decide to take a short cut back to their home by going down an alleyway. They did not account for a man holding them at gunpoint. The mugger shoots Thomas and Martha Wayne leaving only Bruce Wayne as the lone survivor. Bruce's sorrow soon turned into anger as he swore that he would train his mind and body until he is ready to make those who causes pain and suffering to others to fear him. That was the night where The Batman was born

Superman

The Man Of Steel

Kryptonian
Clark Kent/Kal-El

Kal-El of Krypton is one of the very few survivers from the total destruction of his home planet. His birth parents put him in the only escape rocket that was working. He crash lands on the planet Earth where he is found by a couple, Jon and Martha Kent, who soon adopted the alien as a human. Clark Kent began to realize that he was not normal as he had these strange abilities though his parents did not treat him differently for them. As he grew older he began learning the morals that he holds onto as he becomes the man of tomorrow, Superman

Diana Prince is daughter of the god Zeus and the Queen of the Amazons Hippolyta thus, making her the Crown Princess of the island of the Amazons, Themyscira. She is raised and taught how to fight by and with her Amazon sisters in arms where she learns to fight. Diana comes to man's world in order to aid the WWII soldier Steve Trevor. She has since continued aiding the people of man's wolrd as the hero Wonder Woman
